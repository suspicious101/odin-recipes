Shahi Paneer

Description

This is also indian recipe which I love the most

Ingredients

  • panner
  • tomatoes
  • cream
  • onions
  • salt
  • oil
  • water

Steps

  1. put oil in container
  2. fry onions and tomatoes until brown
  3. add 100ml water
  4. add salt
  5. add paneer
  6. add cream on top
  7. stir and wait for 30 minutes
  8. enjoy your dish!
