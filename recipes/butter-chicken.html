Butter Chicken

Description

This is one of the most famous recipes in India

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • butter
  • oil
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • salt
  • water

Steps

  1. watch the chicken
  2. boil the chicken
  3. put oil in container
  4. fry onions and tomatoes in the oil until brown
  5. add 1/2 spoon butter
  6. add 100ml water
  7. add salt
  8. wait for 30-60 minutes
  9. enjoy your dish!
